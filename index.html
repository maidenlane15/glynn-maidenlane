🔗 QuickBooks Integration Guide
How it works:
1. A secure proxy (Cloudflare Worker or Vercel function) holds your QB credentials and acts as a bridge between Glynn and QuickBooks.
2. Once the proxy is set up, Glynn gets 24/7 read access to your P&L, revenue, expenses, cash flow, and bank transactions.
3. QuickBooks already links to your bank — so Glynn sees real-time bank data through QB.
What you need to do:
① Go to developer.intuit.com and create a free developer account
② Create a new app → get your Client ID and Client Secret
③ Share those with your TENT developer to set up the proxy
④ Once the proxy URL is ready, enter it below and tap Connect
